Meta Unveils Full AI Ad Automation at NewFronts — Manus AI Now Live in Ads Manager

Meta revealed at NewFronts that its end-to-end AI automation is now operational: advertisers supply a URL and budget, and AI handles creative generation, audience targeting, placement optimization, and bidding — with Advantage+ Shopping campaigns delivering 22% higher ROAS than manual setups. The company embedded its $2B Manus AI acquisition directly into Ads Manager (rolled out to all advertisers in February), while simultaneously tapping signals from 1B+ monthly Meta AI users across WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram, and Facebook. For a Director of Paid Media, this is both a massive efficiency unlock and a creative control question — as agencies that restructured Advantage+ campaigns around enriched chat signals are reporting 18% ROAS improvements. The strategic challenge: how do you differentiate your clients' brands when every competitor has access to the same automation stack?

Canada's Digital Ad Market Hits $13.98B in 2026, Tracking Toward $21.14B by 2029 — Retail Media the Fastest Growing Segment

Canada's digital advertising market is projected to reach US$13.98 billion in 2026 (up 13% year over year) and expand at a 14.8% CAGR to approximately US$21.14 billion by 2029, according to a new Globe Newswire market report. As retail media network partnerships and domestic alternatives scale up, agencies that position clients ahead of that shift will have a competitive story to tell.
